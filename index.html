BIRTH DAY CALCULATOR

This web application takes a user's birthday and calculates the day of the week they were born and then depending on their gender outputs their Akan Name as described below

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. Here are Ghanian day names.

Female Names

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday:  Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama

Male Names

  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday:  Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame
